Product Description

The iPhone 15 Pro represents the pinnacle of smartphone technology. Crafted from aerospace-grade titanium, it's both lighter and more durable than ever before. The revolutionary A17 Pro chip delivers unprecedented performance for gaming, photography, and productivity.

Featuring a stunning 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology, the iPhone 15 Pro offers an immersive visual experience. The advanced camera system includes a 48MP Main camera, 12MP Ultra Wide camera, and 12MP Telephoto camera with 3x optical zoom.

With USB-C for the first time, the iPhone 15 Pro offers faster data transfer speeds and compatibility with a wider range of accessories. The Action button replaces the traditional Ring/Silent switch, providing customizable shortcuts for your most-used features.
